--- layout: project project: 'trashbin' projTitle: Trashbin projBio: "Trashbin is a mixed-case typeface inspired by a graffiti sign that was sighted upon during a walk on Canal St. The initial grafitti only consisted of the letters: C, A, N, + L, in which the rest of the letterforms were inspired from.

The typespecimen brings the typeface back to it's original sighting, graffitied on a wall, surrounded by custom SA and design inspired stickers. You can purchase the typeface here and all proceeds will go back to the Chinatown Partnership

This short type exploration was made during my time at Sunday Afternoon and is part of the project CANAL Issue 2." projYear: 2023 projCredits: "
